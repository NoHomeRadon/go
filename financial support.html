Financial Assistance Programs

  • Do you meet the federal criteria of being in poverty?

    Seek help from State Housing Finance Agencies (HFAs). HFAs are state-chartered authorities established to help meet the affordable housing needs of the residents of their states. Find the local branch of HFAs and they would finance you for home repairs, such as radon mitigation.

  • Do not meet the federal criteria of being in poverty? Check out Self Help Credit Union!

    Self-Help is a family of five nonprofit organizations aiming at raising resources and advocating for economic opportunity. Visit their home page and apply a loan to fix your home.
