Test It

As Soon As Possible.

It can be in your home!

Radon is colorless, odorless and tasteless. You won't know their very existence unless you do a radon test.

Want to get a test kit?

Smart move! Here are several ways by which you could get access to radon test.

  • The National Radon Program Services at Kansas State University provides discounted test kits available to purchase online. You may also use a coupon and get the kit at even more affordable price.

  • Contact your state radon contact to check services about radon measurement and radon mitigations/reductions in your state. They should have programs about radon test and mitigation. Some states offer free or discounted test kits to the public.

  • You could also find professional test kits in some online stores.
